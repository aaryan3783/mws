Mission And Vision

Mission

The mission of the Marjum Welfare Society is to make our state and the nation educationally sound, socially viable, emotionally strong, culturally stable, economically self- sufficient, environmentally sustainable, mentally strong, physically healthy and spiritually elevated.

For the sake of national unity and integrity we strive to insert national feeling among masses through various programmes and activities. For the protection and preservation of the cultural identity of the Himalayan states we strive to arrange various types of the programmes by taking supports from the state and the nation. To instill the feeling of national integrity and international understanding among the people our society will strive and adopt some bold steps. To make our tribal community educationally advanced the Teacher Education institutes would be opened so as to generate more and more teachers in this locality which may impart education to the ignorant and illiterate people. For the cause of environmental upgradation the organic farming will be encouraged. To harness the strength of rising generation we would try to motivate them through various programmes and activities.

Vision

“To make a just society full of hope and aspiration where people could understand their identity, preserve and promote their culture and try to extirpate ignorance, poverty, discrimination from the country and the world.”
